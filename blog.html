An unfulfilled desire

Put as much effort as you want,
But there'll always be a piece missing of your puzzle,
A forever chasing ghost that will possess you and haunt,
Desire, is the name of that wild on which no one can ever put a muzzle.
Roots want to reach out to the core,
Clouds and birds want to reach out to the sun,
Rainbow's not satisfied with the spectrum, it wants more .
Plants and trees wish they could jump and run .
Ocean wishes more water,
Land wishes more parts , may be a quarter.
Some have got everything they want ,
But their pot is never gonna fill up ,
Some wish they had parents, that's all they want,
While some want to get rid of those who gave them birth.
Many want to fill a cave full of currency notes,
While many struggle everyday to get those life boats.
May be the emojis want to come alive ,
The paper plane wants to fly up to space ,
May be the balloon wants to go scuba dive,
Snowman wants to enjoy summer with a red face .
Everyone out there may be living or non- living,
Has an incomplete part of itself,
A door which opens up to endless doors,
A never ending road called unfulfilled desire ,an alarm that always keeps ringing.
